# Finding a Motif in DNA

## [Rosalind Problem](https://rosalind.info/problems/subs/){:target="_blank"}

**Given**: Two DNA strings $s$ and $t$ (each of length at most 1 kbp).

**Return**: All locations of $t$ as a substring of $s$.

### Sample Dataset

```
GATATATGCATATACTT
ATAT
```

### Sample Output

```
2 4 10
```
